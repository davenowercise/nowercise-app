Foundation Strength Program

A comprehensive strength training program using barbell and resistance band exercises for cancer patients

Day 1 - Lower Body Focus

Band-Resisted Goblet Squat

3 sets of 12 reps
Goblet squat with resistance band - focus on controlled movement

Barbell Hip Thrust

3 sets of 10 reps
Hip thrust - squeeze glutes at top position

Banded Hip Extension

2 sets of 15 reps
Banded hip extension - maintain neutral spine

Day 2 - Upper Body Focus

Band-Resisted Pushup

3 sets of 10 reps
Band-resisted pushups - adjust resistance as needed

Band-Resisted Scapular Pushup

3 sets of 12 reps
Scapular pushups for shoulder stability

Day 3 - Full Body Focus

Barbell Front Squat to Box

3 sets of 8 reps
Barbell front squat to box - control descent and drive up
