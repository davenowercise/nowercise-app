#### 1. **Expand Content Source Support**
To keep it maintainable and modular, consider moving `guidelineSections` to a separate `data/guidelines.ts` or `.json` file. This makes future content edits non-code dependent.

#### 2. **Add ARIA Attributes for Accessibility**
Add `aria-expanded` and `aria-controls` to your toggle buttons:

```jsx
<button
  onClick={() => toggleSection(section.id)}
  aria-expanded={isOpen}
  aria-controls={`${section.id}-content`}
  className="..."
>
```

And assign a matching ID to the collapsible content:

```jsx
{isOpen && (
  <div id={`${section.id}-content`} className="px-4 pb-4">
```
